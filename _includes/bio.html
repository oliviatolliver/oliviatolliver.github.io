About Me

Cornell University · Mechanical Engineering

Hello! I'm Olivia Tolliver, a Mechanical Engineering student at Cornell University (Class of 2027) with a minor in Computer Science. I'm drawn to problems that require both rigorous engineering and creative thinking, the kind where the solution has to be designed, built, tested, and rethought from scratch. My experience spans robotics, biomedical devices, and mechanical design, and I've had the opportunity to contribute to research, competition teams, and coursework as both a builder and a mentor. I'm stepping into a subteam lead role on Cornell's Mars Rover Project Team in Fall 2026, and I've served as a Teaching Assistant for Cornell's introductory mechanical design course, two roles that reflect how much I value the human side of engineering alongside the technical. Outside the lab and machine shop, I'm active in NSBE and Alpha Kappa Alpha, where I serve in leadership roles centered on community, mentorship, and professional development.I'm most energized by work that sits at the intersection of mechanical systems and real-world impact, and I'm always looking for the next problem worth solving.
